ID: ASGN2302

Insurance Premium Prediction:

Dataset: Features include policyholder age, gender, health metrics, smoking status, coverage type, previous claims, annual income, and geographical location.

Objective: 
    Build a regression model to predict insurance premiums based on the provided features. 
    The goal is to estimate insurance costs accurately, helping insurance companies set fair and competitive premiums.

dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/schran/insurance-premium-prediction/

Objective: Build a regression model to predict the lifetime value of a customer based on their historical behaviour.
