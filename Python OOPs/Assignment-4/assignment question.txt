Assignment-4

ID: ASGN1006

1.  Define two mixins:
    a.  AppointmentMixin for managing appointments.
        Add two methods schedule_appointment (self, appointment), cancel_appointment (self, appointment_id)
    b.  PatientMixin for managing patients.
        Add two methods add_patient(self, patient), remove_patient(self, patient_id)
2.  Define HealthcareSystem class class that inherits from both mixins. This class combines the functionality of both mixins.

3.  Create Appointment and Patient classes to represent appointments and patients.

4.  Demonstrate the usage of the HealthcareSystem by scheduling appointments, adding patients, canceling appointments, removing a patient and then printing the appointments and patient details.
